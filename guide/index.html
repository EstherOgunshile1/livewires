Your Live Wires Playground

Welcome to your very own Live Wires project. Live Wires is a framework made with Sass and Mixture that allows you to create responsive grey-box wireframes in HTML & SCSS. Create your wireframes right here. Then customize them, style them, and share them as living low-fidelity prototypes instead of static files you sweat over and later toss away.

Today we stop making trash and, instead, start building living, breathing beauties that will look and act the way they were imagined.

HINT: When you're done, refine your styles and layouts, and rejoice. You now have a complete foundation for a fully-functioning prototype without any wasted time or code.
